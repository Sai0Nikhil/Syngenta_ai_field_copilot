# AI Field Copilot — Demo Video Script

**Total runtime:** 2 minutes (120 seconds)
**Voice:** one narrator, calm and confident, ~145 words/min
**On-screen text language:** English
**Background music:** soft acoustic, fades out under voiceover

## Tips for the recorder

- Record the screen at 1920 × 1080. Crop to the Streamlit window during shots 3-7.
- Pause for one full second when zooming into a retailer card so judges' eyes catch the score badge.
- Keep cursor visible and slow — judges should follow what's being clicked.
- For shot 5 (heatmap), record at least 8 seconds of map idle so the pan looks intentional, not jittery.
- Master the audio: -16 LUFS, no background music louder than -28 dB.
